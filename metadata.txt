about=PlanX CartoLab is a publication-grade thematic mapping engine for QGIS. It integrates native 2.5D building styling with height-field extrusion, adaptive Geometric Interval Classification (GIC), continuous-area cartogram distortion, Value-by-Alpha uncertainty visualisation, ridge-line vector mesh generation, dot-density and Flannery proportional-symbol mapping, hexbin point aggregation, pole-of-inaccessibility (polylabel) label points, coordinate graticules, choropleth normalization and rate calculation, stacked isometric layout compositing, and floating HTML chart annotations. Built for academic-grade cartography within the PlanX ecosystem.
